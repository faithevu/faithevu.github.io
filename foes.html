Beware of Food Foes!

A little is fine, a lot is not!

Cookie

This is Cookie!

Don't eat too many processed desserts!

Desserts like cookies and cakes contain a lot of unhealtly saturated fats! Eating too much can raises cholesterol, affecting you heart health. This can also lead to weight gain, which affects digestion and energy.

Avoid or limit eating fried foods, baked desserts, and overly cheesy foods!

Candy

This is Candy!

Too many sweets can hurt your teeth!

Unlike fruits, candies have added sugars which are often unnatural! Eating too many sweets can affect your mood, harm your heart, cause diabetes, and damage your teeth!

Avoid or limit candy, soft drinks/sweetened drinks, as well as baked and dairy desserts in your diet!
