Meet our Powerful Proteins!

Flex your powers with thes Powerful Protein Pals!

Chic

Meet Chic!

Chicken is a great type of lean protein!

Like many other animal-based protein, chicken help build healthy tissue, bones, and muscles! Be mindful that other meats or poultry may have more unhealthy fats than others.

1 small cooked chicken breast is about 3 oz-equiv of protein!

Sammy

Meet Sammy the Salmon!

Sammy is super smart!

Along with being a powerful protein, the omega-3 fats in salmon and other fish are goof for your brain and heart.

1 ounce of cooked finfish like salmon equal 1 oz-equiv of protein!

Eddie

Meet Eddie Edamame!

Eddie is a plant-based protein!

Edamame and tofu lowers cholesterol, which is good for blood flow! They help your heart and brain function better!

¼ cup of tofu equals 1 oz-equiv to protein!
