Meet your new Fruity Friends!

These berries are lower in sugar and calories compared to other fruits! They are good for your heart, brain and digestion.

Sweetie

Meet Sweetie the Strawberry!

Sweetie is bursting with vitamin C!

Strawberries are known as the beauty fruit, good for your skin and full of vitamin C, lowering chance of getting sick.

About 8 big strawberries equals 1 cup of fruit!

BB

Meet BB the Blueberry!

Boost your brain power with BB!

Blueberries are one of the healthiest fruits, boosting heart health and brain function. They are full of antioxidants, which protects your cells from being damaged, guarding from aging and diseases such as cancer!

Did you know ⅓ cup of dried blueberries equals 1 cup of fruit!

Raspberries are a great snack fresh or frozen!
