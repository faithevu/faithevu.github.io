Meet these Vital Veggies!

Be fit and fast with Vital Veggies!

Broc

Meet Broc the Broccoli!

Broc will help you stay strong!

Broccoli boosts immunity to keep you healthy! It's a great source of fiber and protein!

A cup of broccoli, whether fresh or frozen, counts as 1 cup of vegetables!

Kyle

Meet Kyle the Kale!

Leafy greens like Kyle are just as important!

Kale has calcium and iron to keep your bones strong, vitamin C to help your immunity fight infections, and has fiber to keep your gut healthy!

A cup of cooked kale or two cups of fresh kale counts as 1 cup of vegetables!
